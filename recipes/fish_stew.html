African Fish Stew



It is fast to cook. Good for the whole family. Can be served with ugali which is made of corn floor.
The family will enjoy the Meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup seafood stock
  • Â¼ cup tomato paste
  • 1 head bok choy, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon gumbo file powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 saffron threads, or to taste
  • 3 pounds tilapia fillets

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Add onion; cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add seafood stock and tomato paste. Add bok choy, garlic, gumbo file, salt, pepper, and saffron;
  4. ook and stir until well incorporated, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add tilapia.
  6. Let simmer until flavors blend and fish flakes easily with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
