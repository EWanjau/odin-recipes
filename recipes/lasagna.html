Beautiful Lasagna


Description

This introduces a simple way to make a sweet lasagna. It may take 30 minutes to get the ingredients together, 30 minutes to cook that makes it 1 hour maximum time. The quantities will be served for 7 servings. Enjoy

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces lasagna noodles
  • 1 and half pounds lean ground beef
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef. Using a fork, mix cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, parsley, salt and pepper. In a separate bowl combine tomato paste and tomato sauce.
  4. n a 9x13 inch baking pan, layer noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixture and cottage cheese mixture.
  5. Two layers of each will fit. Cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove tin foil the last 8 minutes of baking time.
