Scrabble Eggs



It is fast to cook. Good for the whole family. Can be served with ugali which is made of corn floor.
The family will enjoy the Meal!

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon bacon grease, or as needed
  • ⅛ cup bacon bits
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Crack 7 whole eggs into a medium bowl. Separate remaining egg.
  2. Add yolk to the bowl; reserve white for another use. Pour in milk
  3. Whisk until thoroughly combined and bubbles form on top.
  4. Melt bacon grease in a pan over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture, followed by bacon bits.
  5. Cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes. Season with salt.
